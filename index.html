About

I am a senior researcher in computer vision and deep learning, affiliated with Huawei Hisilicon. I obtained a dual PhD in Computer Science from City University of Hong Kong and Dalian University of Technology in 2023. I have published at top venues including CVPR, AAAI, IJCV, TIP, and BMVC. As the first author, I got the best student paper runner-up award in BMVC 2020.
